MAJOR PLACES IN SWITZERLAND






The Matterhorn

The Matterhorn, an iconic peak in the Swiss Alps, captivates with its pyramid-shaped silhouette. Towering at 4,478 meters, its majestic presence and challenging ascent have made it a symbol of mountaineering.

Interlaken

Interlaken, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz in the Swiss Alps, captivates with its breathtaking scenery. A haven for outdoor enthusiasts, it offers adventure sports, charming villages, and majestic peaks.

Lucrene

Lucerne, a picturesque Swiss city on Lake Lucerne's shores, blends medieval charm with modern sophistication. Iconic landmarks include Chapel Bridge and Water Tower, creating a captivating blend of history and beauty.
