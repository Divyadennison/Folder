INTERLAKEN

Interlaken is a picturesque town located in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. The name "Interlaken" itself means "between lakes." Surrounded by stunning alpine scenery, Interlaken is a popular destination for tourists seeking outdoor adventures, breathtaking landscapes, and a tranquil atmosphere.
